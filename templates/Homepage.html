Neural Network Based Driver Drowsiness Detection.

Introduction

Aim of our Project

To design a system capable of capturing images and predicting whether the subject (driver) is drowsy or alert and notify the driver by bussing an alarm as soon as the driver is found drowsy.

Project Objective

To ensure driver’s safety and prevent of loss of life. Avoiding damage to road property. To save loss of goods and money invested in it and mainly to prevent road accidents.

Motivation

Driver drowsiness has been one of the major causes of road accidents and can lead to severe physical injuries, deaths and significant economic losses. With motivation to avoid/prevent road accidents, this project involves ensuring driver's safety and preventing loss of life and goods. “To save a life is a real and beautiful thing”
- Vincent Van Gogh
